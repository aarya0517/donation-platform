How It Works

Our platform ensures transparency and accountability in the donation process.

Institutes Request

Needy institutes register and request specific items they need, providing transparency about their requirements.

Donors Contribute

Donors browse requests and choose to fund specific items rather than giving money directly to institutes.

Shopkeepers Fulfill

Registered shopkeepers provide the requested items, which are then delivered to the institutes.
